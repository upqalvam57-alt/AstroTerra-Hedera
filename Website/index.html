About AstroTerra

Our Mission: To make asteroid impact science accessible to everyone. AstroTerra bridges the gap between complex scientific data and public understanding, empowering students, educators, researchers, and policymakers with powerful visualization tools built on real NASA data.

Why AstroTerra Matters

The Problem

Asteroid impacts are rare but catastrophic events. Existing tools are either too technical for public use or too simplified to be scientifically meaningful.

Our Solution

A public-friendly, scientifically accurate simulator that makes complex orbital mechanics and planetary defense strategies accessible to everyone.

The Impact

Educate the public, support research, inform policy decisions, and inspire the next generation of planetary defense scientists.
